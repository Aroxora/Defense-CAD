## Key Findings

### 2025 Parade Strategic Significance

1. **Full Nuclear Triad Display:**
   - First time all three legs shown together (ICBMs, SLBMs, ALBMs)
   - Signals completed second-strike capability
   - JL-3 SLBM demonstrates SSBN operational maturity

2. **Hypersonic Anti-Ship Focus:**
   - YJ-15/17/19/20 series for carrier strike
   - 400-800+ km ranges threaten CSG operating areas
   - A2/AD capabilities extended to second island chain

3. **Information Warfare Emphasis:**
   - New Information Support Force (ISF) debut
   - Cyber, electronic warfare, spectrum control integrated
   - Shift from kinetic to information dominance

4. **AI and Autonomous Systems:**
   - GJ-11 stealth UCAV with AI
   - Loyal wingman drones for J-20 teaming
   - Robotic ground systems for logistics
   - Indicates significant investment in unmanned capabilities

5. **Directed Energy Weapons:**
   - LY-1 laser first public tactical DEW
   - Counter-UAV and missile defense role
   - Technology maturation ahead of most projections
